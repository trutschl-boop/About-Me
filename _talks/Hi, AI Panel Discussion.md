I was part of a panel discussion with a public Q&A following a screening of the movie "Hi, AI" at Lumière Cinema in Maastricht. The panel comprised Dr. Kars Lemmens, Assistant Professor at the School of Business and Economics at Maastricht University, Dr. Massimiliano Simons, Assistant Professor in Philosophy of Technology at Maastricht University, and Dr. Ann Dooms, Associate Professor of digital mathematics at Vrije Universiteit Brussels. It was organised by Dr. Katleen Gabriels, Associate Professor of Philosophy, on behalf of the Maastricht Young Academy.
