In the second ELSA Labs Magazine the labs present themselves in more detail and provide a look behind the curtain of research. The multitude of methodological approaches used and developed by the ELSA Labs are presented. Guest articles shed some light on why the methodologies are so important and how this is important from a policy and governance perspective. We also offer some key learnings on methods from the 6 ELSA Labs.
